The article "OVERCOMING THE CHALLENGE OF PLATFORM COMPATIBILITY IN WEB DEVELOPMENT" written by Krasnova and Vichugov
it touches upon the problem of platform compatibility
At the begginning author defines platform compitability and explains it's main problem
The problem is related to consistent behaviour of application across different platforms
In the main body of an article author shows the main reason that leads to discussable problem
The reason is due to large number of devices and browsers avalable on the market
The next part of article is devoted to possible solution
There author offers web developers to follow latest standards and guidelines and test their application compatibility to ensure that it performs consistently across all platforms 
Also author provide advises to web developers on how to solve issues related to main problem
In conclusion author admits that platform compitability is a challenge and repeats possible solutions
